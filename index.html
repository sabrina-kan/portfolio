The craft

Content design in action

The job isn't writing from scratch — it's rewriting until it's right. Here's what that actually looks like.

⚠ Error message
Before

"Invalid input. Please try again."

After

"Your password needs at least 8 characters — try adding a number or symbol."

□ Empty state
Before

"No items found."

After

"Nothing here yet — add your first task to start tracking your progress."

⚖ Legal/compliance copy
Before

"Complete mandatory identity verification as required by FCA regulations."

After

"We need to verify your identity — it's a legal requirement and takes about 2 minutes."
