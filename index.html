About me

I'm a content designer with 6+ years of experience shaping how products speak to people. I've worked embedded on cross-functional teams at startups and scale-ups, bridging the gap between product, design, and engineering.

My process starts with listening—to users, stakeholders, and the product itself—then translating messy realities into clear, consistent language that actually works.

When I'm not sweating the words, I'm probably thinking about information architecture, teaching a content writing workshop, or hunting for a great flat white.
