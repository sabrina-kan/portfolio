Onboarding copyKnox Suite · 2025

Knox registration flow — product selection

Onboarding copy for a multi-product platform is a content IA problem as much as a writing problem. Users arrive with different levels of purchase intent and different product knowledge. Each option needed to be specific enough to self-select accurately, without assuming the reader already knows what Knox Suite or Knox Configure means.
