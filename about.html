About me

Hi there, I'm Sabrina! I'm a content designer with 7+ years of experience helping product teams find the right words.

I came to content design the long way, and it shows.

Most content designers come from writing. I started with a more structural approach: managing websites, organizing knowledge bases, and writing technical documentation before I ever wrote a line of UX copy.

I think in systems before I think in sentences, and I understand the technical constraints that most writers don't see. Today, I'm bringing that full stack of experience to content design, where structure, clarity, and user empathy all have to work together."

How I work

I prefer to be embedded early — at the discovery stage rather than at the "can you review this copy?" stage. The earlier I'm involved, the more I can shape the experience rather than translate someone else's decisions into words.

I work closely with research, often running my own sessions when I need to understand how users talk about a product domain. I use content audits as a diagnostic tool, not just as a way to catalogue existing copy. And I document everything — because a content decision that lives only in my head is a content system waiting to collapse.
